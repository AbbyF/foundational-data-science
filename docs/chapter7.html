Foundational Data Science

Chapter 7 Wrap-up

The goal of this short book is provide a short introduction into the wonderful world of data science, from a statistics view point. Using the simplest of statistical techniques, from summary statistics to graphics, we can highlight unusual and interesting patterns in our data. These techniques lay the analysis foundations for more advanced techniques.
